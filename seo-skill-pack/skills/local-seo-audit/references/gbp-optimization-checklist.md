# Google Business Profile Optimization Checklist

## Core Information

### Business Name
- Use exact legal/real-world business name
- Do not add keywords, locations, or taglines
- Example: "Bright Smile Dental" NOT "Bright Smile Dental - Best Dentist in Austin TX"
- Exception: if location is part of legal name (e.g., "Austin Family Dentistry")

### Categories

#### Primary Category
- Choose the MOST SPECIFIC category that describes the core business
- Google offers ~4,000+ categories — search for the most precise one
- Examples:
  - "Dentist" → better: "Cosmetic Dentist" or "Pediatric Dentist"
  - "Restaurant" → better: "Italian Restaurant" or "Sushi Restaurant"
  - "Lawyer" → better: "Personal Injury Attorney" or "Immigration Lawyer"

#### Secondary Categories (up to 9)
- Add all relevant categories
- Include service-specific categories
- Example for a dental office:
  - Primary: "Cosmetic Dentist"
  - Secondary: "Dental Clinic", "Emergency Dental Service", "Teeth Whitening Service", "Dental Implants Provider"

### Address
- Must match exactly across all platforms
- Use USPS-standardized format for US businesses
- Spell out or abbreviate consistently (St vs Street — pick one)
- Include suite/unit number if applicable
- Service-area businesses: can hide address, show service area

### Phone
- Use local phone number (not toll-free for local businesses)
- Format consistently: (555) 123-4567 or 555-123-4567
- Must be direct to the business (not call center)
- Add additional phones in GBP (tracking number can go here)

### Website URL
- Link to homepage for single-location businesses
- Link to specific location page for multi-location
- Use UTM parameters for tracking: `?utm_source=google&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp`

## Description (750 characters max)

### Structure
1. **Opening sentence** — what the business does + location (include primary keyword)
2. **Services/specialties** — top 3-5 services
3. **Differentiators** — what makes this business unique
4. **Call to action** — invite customers to take next step

### Do's
- Include primary and secondary keywords naturally
- Mention service area/neighborhood names
- Highlight awards, certifications, years in business
- Use complete sentences

### Don'ts
- No keyword stuffing
- No URLs (they're not clickable)
- No all-caps
- No promotional language ("BEST PRICES!")
- No phone numbers (they're not clickable)

### Example
```
Bright Smile Dental has been providing comprehensive dental care in North Austin
since 2008. Our services include cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, teeth
whitening, and emergency dental care. Led by Dr. Jane Smith (DDS, 15+ years
experience), our team uses the latest digital dental technology. We're proud
to be rated 4.9 stars by over 300 patients. Schedule your appointment today to
experience patient-centered dental care in a comfortable environment.
```

## Hours

### Regular Hours
- Set accurate hours for all 7 days
- If closed on a day, mark as "Closed" (don't leave blank)
- Use 12-hour format for clarity

### Special Hours
- Set for all major holidays BEFORE they arrive
- Common holidays to pre-set: New Year's, MLK Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Columbus Day, Veterans Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, Christmas, New Year's Eve
- Add custom special hours for events, renovations, etc.

### More Hours
- Add specific service hours if different from general:
  - "Drive-through hours"
  - "Delivery hours"
  - "Senior hours"
  - "Happy hour"

## Photos & Media

### Minimum Photo Set (10+ recommended)
1. **Logo** — square, clear, professional
2. **Cover photo** — best representation of business (1024x576 recommended)
3. **Exterior** — 2-3 shots (daytime, signage, parking)
4. **Interior** — 3-5 shots (reception, work areas, atmosphere)
5. **Team** — 2-3 photos (staff, owner, team working)
6. **Products/Services** — 3-5 photos of work, menu items, products

### Photo Best Practices
- JPEG or PNG format
- Between 10KB and 5MB per photo
- Minimum resolution: 720x720
- No stock photos — use real photos
- Add photos regularly (weekly/biweekly)
- Geo-tag photos with EXIF data when possible
- Name files descriptively: `austin-dental-office-reception.jpg`

### Videos
- 30 seconds to 5 minutes
- Max 75MB at 720p resolution
- Tour of business, service demonstrations, customer testimonials

## Posts

### Post Types
1. **Update** — general news, tips, behind-the-scenes
2. **Event** — upcoming events with date/time
3. **Offer** — promotions, discounts (with expiration date)

### Post Best Practices
- Post minimum 1x per week (2-3x ideal)
- Include a photo or image with every post
- Add a CTA button (Book, Order online, Learn more, Sign up, Call)
- Keep text 150-300 words
- Include keywords naturally
- Track which posts drive the most actions

## Q&A Section

### Seed Questions
Pre-populate with 10-15 most commonly asked questions:
- Business hours and location questions
- Service/product availability
- Pricing or payment questions
- Parking/accessibility
- COVID protocols (if applicable)
- Booking/appointment process

### Monitoring
- Check Q&A weekly for new customer questions
- Answer promptly (within 24 hours)
- Upvote your own answers so they appear first
- Flag spam or inappropriate questions

## Reviews Strategy

### Getting Reviews
- Ask satisfied customers directly (in person, via email, via SMS)
- Create a short review link: `https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=[PLACE_ID]`
- Add review link to: email signatures, receipts, follow-up emails, website
- Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy)
- Don't gate reviews (ask for rating first, then send to Google only if positive)

### Responding to Reviews
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24-48 hours
- **Positive reviews**: Thank by name, mention specific service, invite back
- **Negative reviews**: Apologize, take responsibility, offer to resolve offline
- Include keywords naturally in responses
- Keep responses professional and genuine

### Review Response Templates

**Positive (customize each):**
```
Thank you [Name] for the wonderful review! We're glad you had a great
experience with [specific service]. Our team takes pride in [relevant value].
We look forward to seeing you again!
```

**Negative (customize each):**
```
[Name], thank you for sharing your experience. We sincerely apologize for
[acknowledge issue]. This doesn't reflect our standards for [service area].
Please contact us at [phone/email] so we can make this right.
```

- Curbside pickup

### From the Business

## Multi-Location Considerations

For businesses with multiple locations:
- Each location gets its own GBP listing
- Each listing links to its specific location page (not homepage)
- Maintain consistent branding across all listings
- Set up a location group for easier management
- Use bulk upload for 10+ locations
