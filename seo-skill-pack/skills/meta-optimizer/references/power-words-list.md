## Numbers That Drive Clicks

### List Numbers (odd numbers perform better)
3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 13, 15, 17, 21, 25, 27, 31, 50, 101

### Percentage & Data
- Specific percentages: "73% of...", "2.5x faster"
- Round numbers for impressiveness: "$1 million", "10,000 customers"
- Precise numbers for credibility: "37 strategies", "14.7% increase"

### Time References
- "in 5 minutes", "in 30 days", "24/7"
- "2026" (current year is strongest)
- "Updated [Month Year]"

## Emotional Trigger Formulas

### Fear of Missing Out (FOMO)
```
Don't Miss: [Keyword] [Benefit] | [Brand]
[Number] [Keyword] Mistakes You're Making | [Brand]
Stop [Bad Action]: [Keyword] Guide | [Brand]
```

### Curiosity Gap
```
The [Keyword] [Noun] Nobody Talks About | [Brand]
Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong About [Keyword] | [Brand]
[Number] [Keyword] Secrets [Authority] Won't Tell You | [Brand]
```

### Social Proof
```
[Keyword] Trusted by [Number]+ [Users/Businesses] | [Brand]
[Number]-Star Rated [Keyword] in [Location] | [Brand]
Why [Number] [Professionals] Choose [Product] | [Brand]
```

### Problem-Solution
```
[Problem]? Here's How to [Solution] | [Brand]
Fix [Problem] with [Keyword] — [Time Frame] | [Brand]
Struggling with [Problem]? [Number] Proven [Solutions] | [Brand]
```

## Words to AVOID in Meta Tags

### Spam Triggers
- "Click here", "Buy now!!!", "AMAZING!!!", "100% FREE!!!"
- Excessive punctuation (!!!, ???, $$$)
- ALL CAPS words (occasional caps OK for emphasis)
- Misleading claims ("Guaranteed #1 ranking")

### Weak / Filler Words
- "Very", "Really", "Just", "Things", "Stuff"
- "Click here to learn more"
- "Welcome to our website"
- "Page 1", "Home"

### Google May Rewrite Titles That
- Are too long (>60 chars)
- Don't match page content
- Are keyword-stuffed
- Use boilerplate patterns
- Have incorrect or misleading claims
- Add site name when not needed
